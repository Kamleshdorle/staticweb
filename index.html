Why Choose Us?

Ayurveda is a proven ancient science with almost no side effects. Diseases which cannot be cured by any other means can be cured by Ayurveda. Ayurveda is part of our proud culture and along with Yoga has cured thousands of patients in the last many centuries.

Our objective of making people happy and healthy through authentic Ayurvedic treatment delivered at their doorstep is a direct response to the growing ailments and disorders such as diabetes, arthritis, asthma, obesity, hypertension, spondylitis, piles, skin problems, sexual disorders, etc.

We are committed to making Ayurveda accessible to all, creating a wide range of natural medicine for different diseases, as well as to prevent and protect against illness.

The shift towards a holistic and natural lifestyle for better health does not just pertain to medical illness or medicines, but also includes everyday personal care. This is why our collection of health products also includes herbal skin andÂ hair care products.

Three qualities or doshas form important characteristics of the body constitution. Vata dosha reflects the qualities of space and air elements and governs our body movements. Pita dosha is made of fire and water elements and governs digestion, nutrition, metabolism and body temperature. Kapha dosha reflects the qualities of earth and water elements and governs our bones, muscles, teeth and everything dense in our body. These are biological energies which govern all physical and mental processes and its balance can vary from person to person, influenced by genetics and lifestyle factors.
